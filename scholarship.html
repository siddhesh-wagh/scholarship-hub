What is a Scholarship

A scholarship is a financial gift given to students to help them pay for their education. It's like winning a prize for doing well in school or having a special talent. Unlike a loan, you don't have to pay back a scholarship. It's free money to help you cover things like tuition fees, books, and living expenses while you're studying.

Scholarships can come from different sources, like schools, governments, or private organizations. They're awarded based on things like good grades, academic achievements, financial need, or unique skills or interests.

Overall, scholarships are a way to support students in pursuing their education dreams by easing the financial burden and providing recognition for their hard work and abilities.
