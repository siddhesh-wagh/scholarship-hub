Pre-Matric Scholarship

The Pre-Matric Scholarship is a government initiative aimed at providing financial assistance to students belonging to minority communities in India at the pre-matriculation level. This scholarship scheme is designed to support students from economically weaker sections to continue their education without facing financial constraints.

Key Features

  • Eligibility: Students belonging to minority communities such as Muslims, Christians, Sikhs, Buddhists, Jains, and Parsis are eligible to apply for this scholarship. The scheme is primarily targeted at students from economically disadvantaged families.
  • Scholarship Amount: The scholarship provides financial assistance to cover various educational expenses such as tuition fees, admission fees, maintenance allowance, etc. The amount granted varies depending on factors such as the student's class, type of institution, and location.
  • Application Process: Interested students need to apply for the Pre-Matric Scholarship through designated online portals or offline application forms provided by the respective state or central government authorities. The application process typically involves providing details such as personal information, family income, educational qualifications, etc.
  • Selection Criteria: Selection for the scholarship is based on various criteria, including the applicant's academic merit, economic background, and community status. Students need to meet the specified eligibility criteria to qualify for the scholarship.
  • Renewal: In most cases, the scholarship needs to be renewed for each academic year by meeting certain criteria, such as maintaining a minimum level of academic performance and attendance.
  • Empowerment: The Pre-Matric Scholarship aims to empower minority students by providing them with equal opportunities for education, thereby enhancing their chances of pursuing higher studies and achieving their career aspirations.

Maintenance allowance for students studying in Sainiki School.

About Scheme

Department Name

Social Justice and Special Assistance

Overview

    SC students studying in govt or private Sainik school

Benefits

    1)For Satara,Nashik and Pune govt Sainik schools with respect to fees structure shared from school education department.
    2) For private sainik school Rs.15000.00 will be disbursed per annum.
    3) Disbursement will happen in two instalment.
    4) Note: No extra benefit for more than 10 months. For less than 10 months, the beneficiary will be paid for only the no. of months he attended school from the date of admission.

Eligibilty

    1) Students must belong to the SC caste.
    2) Students must be studying in a govt or private Sainik School.
    3) Parents/Guardian Income should be less than or equal to 2,50,000
    4) Student must be a resident of Maharashtra.
    5)For second instalment 60% attendance is mandatory of current academic year.
    6) All board student except Self Finance student school are eligible for the scheme.
    7) If student gets fail for the year he/she is not eligible till he/she pass the class.

Renewal Policy

    Renewal applications same eligibility criteria of new application need to follow.

Document Required

    1) Domicile Certificate.
    2) Income Certificate
    3) Caste Certificate.

Savitribai Phule Scholarship for Scheduled Caste girl’s students studying in 5th to 7th STD

About Scheme

Department Name

Social Justice and Special Assistance

Overview

    1) This Scheme is for SC girl students studying in class from 5th to 10th in govt. schools or govt. aided schools.

Benefits

    1) Students studying in class 5-7 std. get Rs.60 per month for up to 10months.
    2) Students studying in class 8-10th std get Rs.100 per month for up to 10 months
    3) Disbursement will happen in two instalments.
    Note: No extra benefit for more than 10 months. For less than 10 months, the beneficiary will be paid for only the no. of months he attended school from the date of admission.

Eligibilty

    1) The student should be a girl child and should belong to the Schedule Caste category
    2) No Income Limit
    3) Student must be a resident of Maharashtra
    4) All board student except Self-Financed student school are eligible for the scheme
    5)The student should be studying in Government schools or Government aided schools.
    6) If student gets fail for the year he is not eligible till she pass the class
    7) For the second installment, attendance is 60% mandatory for the current academic year.

Renewal Policy

    Renewal applications same eligibility criteria of new application need to follow.

Documents Required

    1)Domicile Certificate.
    2)Caste Certificate
